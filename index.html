Project NAINA

23 Mar, 2016

It stands for :- New Artificial Intelligence with Natural Augmentation. The aim of the project was to make a cardboard app in which 3-D drawing could be done. This idea can then be extended to allow 3-D modelling of robots and other structures. Tracking the users’s hand motion. Technology used : IMU 6050 along with Arduino UNO. Difficulty in transferring the data obtained to the cardboard android app made me drop the idea of using the sensor.
